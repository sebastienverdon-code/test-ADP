About

Sebastian Verdon is a Switzerland-based photographer specializing in the photographic documentation of exhibitions and the reproduction of artworks. In dialogue with artists and institutions, his work aims to create a precise and objective visual archive of artworks and their exhibition contexts.

All images courtesy the Artists, the Art Spaces, the Galleries, the Museums and Sebastian Verdon.
